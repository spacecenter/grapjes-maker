Grapjes Maker

AI Comedy Video Generator

Notice: Grapjes Maker is a personal web application that runs locally on the user's own machine. It is not a publicly hosted service.

Grapjes Maker is a personal, locally-run web application that generates short comedy videos and allows users to publish them directly to TikTok. The app runs locally on the user's own machine.

TikTok Integration AI-Powered Personal Use

How It Works

1

Provide Your Content

Enter the text content you want to visualize as a comedy video.

2

Review the Script

Grapjes Maker generates a video script based on your content for your approval.

3

Generate Video

After approval, Grapjes Maker creates your video clip automatically.

4

Publish to TikTok

When ready, publish your video directly to your TikTok account with one click.

Features

  • AI Joke Video Generator — Create short comedy videos instantly using artificial intelligence
  • TikTok Integration — Connect your account and publish videos directly to TikTok drafts
  • Fast Video Creation — Generate and publish in seconds
